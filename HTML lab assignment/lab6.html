Definitions:

Coffee

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from certain Coffea species. Once ripe, coffee berries are picked, processed, and dried. Dried coffee seeds (referred to as "beans") are roasted to varying degrees, depending on the desired flavor.

Milk

Milk is a nutrient-rich liquid food produced by the mammary glands of mammals. It provides essential nutrients such as proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals necessary for the development and growth of young mammals.
